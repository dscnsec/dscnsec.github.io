Frequently Asked Questions

What is DSC?

Developer Students’ Club is a Google Developers program for university students, designed to help them build their development skills in various domains. It is an officially recognized Students’ Club.

What do we do?

Developer Students Club-NSEC is a Google recognized student body at NSEC which directly reports to Google Developers for their activities and is supported by the tech giant. DSC-NSEC conducts workshops, seminars and other fun activities which help the students connect with Google. And we give Google interview referrals too!

How can I be a part?

Just register in our website and its completely free of cost.

Is this for girls?

One-fourth of the total members in the club are girls who are working efficiently alongside their male counterparts.

When I should apply?

It is always better to start early so that you can get an edge over others.
